The Rules of my Anagram Game.


What to do?:

The game is an anagram word input, where you have a word at the top of the screen jumbled up and you have to input what you think is the correct word.


Timer:

The timer counts up and you get more words correct. The game stops when you get all the words right.

Warning & Praise:

There is a warning saying that you got a word wrong and to try again. There is also an well done after you get each word correct.
